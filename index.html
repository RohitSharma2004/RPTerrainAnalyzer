How It Works

1

Image Capture

The app uses your device's camera to capture high-resolution terrain images from above.

2

Analysis

Advanced algorithms process the image to detect flat areas, edges, and terrain features.

3

ML Classification

Our trained model determines if the terrain is safe for landing with high confidence.

4

Command Transmission

Landing decisions are sent to connected controllers via Bluetooth for autonomous operation.
